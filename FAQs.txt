Why Relational DB?

NoSQL is for data which is semi structured and dynamic, like products in ecommerce where dresses can have sizes but not all other products. Although NoSQL is faster and provides higher performance, since our website takes care of only houses and it is better to use SQL since SQL is used for more complex queries like JOINs and etc which we will use in our website.
